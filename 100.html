I'm at the shores of a beach, looking out, the waves waltz forward. Nobody can stop them. Beyond me the sky flickers, when it's white, I feel nothing. When its purple, fireworks cloud the horizon and stand static until I turn my head the other direction, I feel like I'm at the end of a very long book, I'm at the final act of something that I've only recently realized is ending. I didn't know before. Nobody told me. All the cues are there, nothings stopping the march of time now.

I wonder how this final act is going to play out.
